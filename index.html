Brief History

Established in 1958 by Mr. A.Eylon and Mr. D.Goffer. Moked personnel has more than 40 years of experience in the field of precision machining. As a pioneer of this industrial field in Israel, Moked has made an important contribution to the growth of this relatively young country's technology.

In recent years the firm has expanded its activities, while maintaining its position as one of the leading manufacturers of high precision mechanical parts in Israel. Today the firm is managed by the second generation of owners. It has a consistent yearly growth rate of 20%, mainly through the production of aerospace parts for international companies.

Moked is a private company owned by Mr. D.Goffer and Mr. A. Eylon.

Aerospace parts and subassemblies

High complex aerospace parts and subassemblies, which require a wide range of machining technologies, combined with complicated coating operations. The Raw Material of part's shown are: Aluminum, Aluminum- Bronze and Stainless Steels: SAE 416,17-4 PH

High precision parts

High precision parts, made from high strength and exotic aerospace materials, such as: Titanium, Invar, Kovar, Hymu , Inconel, Discaloy (A286), Waspalloy as well as more standard materials: Copper, 13-8 PH,SS 418(Greek Ascoloy), ect.

Aerospace castings

Machined complex aerospace castings, with tight tolerance dimensions, combined with various coating operations. Raw materials used in the investment castings shown are: SS. 17-4 PH, 15-5 PH, SAE 347 and Aluminum.
